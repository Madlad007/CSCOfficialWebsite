Who We Are

More Than
A Football Club

Canterbury Sporting Club Inc. has been the heartbeat of a vibrant, multicultural community in Sydney's Canterbury area for over 15 years. We bring together families, children, and individuals who share a love for the beautiful game and an even greater love for celebrating the rich tapestry of cultures that make our club unique.
